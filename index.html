How does it work?

This web app is designed to let you explore all your counts of checklists, species and observations on a map. This is computed based on the eBird export file.
My favorite thing with this app is that you can draw any polygon or rectangle (icon on the left side of the screen) on the map and your counts for that zone appears on the bottom of the page!
(Please note that sub-species, slashs, etc are counted as different species for the moment...)
